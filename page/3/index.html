Online Stock Span

问题

Write a class StockSpanner which collects daily price quotes for some stock, and returns the span of that stock’s price for the current day.

The span of the stock’s price today is defined as the maximum number of consecutive days (starting from today and going backwards) for which the price of the stock was less than or equal to today’s price.

For example, if the price of a stock over the next 7 days were $[100, 80, 60, 70, 60, 75, 85]$, then the stock spans would be $[1, 1, 1, 2, 1, 4, 6]$.

就是找数组中，当前数字数字结尾的最大子区间的最大值是当前数字。

Codeforces Vasya and Good Sequences

Posted on 2018-10-23

1058E Vasya and Good Sequences

描述

Vasya有一个由$n$个数字组成的数组，$a_1,a_2,\ldots,a_n$，可以对数组执行如下操作：选择一个数字然后交换它二进制表示中的任意两个bit的位置，
例如可以把$6(\ldots00000000110_2)$转换成$3(\ldots00000000011_2), 12(\ldots000000001100_2), 1026(\ldots10000000010_2)$等等。
可以对任意多的数字执行任意多的这种操作。

如果对一个数组执行上面的操作之后，它所有的数字进行异或操作之后的结果是0，Vasya就认为这个数组是好的。

对于一个给定的数组$a_1,a_2,\ldots,a_n$, Vasya想知道有多少个区间$(l, r) 1 \leq l \leq r \leq n$使得$a_l, a_{l+1}, \ldots, a_r$是好的。

Labyrinth

Posted on 2018-10-18

1064D Labyrinth

Description

You are playing some computer game. One of its levels puts you in a maze consisting of n lines, each of which contains m cells. Each cell either is free or is occupied by an obstacle. The starting cell is in the row r and column c. In one step you can move one square up, left, down or right, if the target cell is not occupied by an obstacle. You can’t move beyond the boundaries of the labyrinth.

Unfortunately, your keyboard is about to break, so you can move left no more than x times and move right no more than y times. There are no restrictions on the number of moves up and down since the keys used to move up and down are in perfect condition.

Now you would like to determine for each cell whether there exists a sequence of moves that will put you from the starting cell to this particular one. How many cells of the board have this property?
